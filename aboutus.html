About Us

Mirchi- Indian Homestyle Restaurant in Edison is located on 1655 Oak Tree Road. Private Party Halls are available for 50 to 600 guests. Mirchi provides authentic Indian homestyle cuisine, Hyderabadi biryani, Chettined style appetizers, Mirchi house specials, traditional Dosa platters and sizzlers, delicious sambhar, chutneys and ethnic pickles from all parts of India to NJ residents. In order to satisfy all customers, Mirchi Indian Restaurant in NJ offers different levels of spice according to your taste buds. Mirchi means 'chillis' in the Indian language. The key ingredient in Indian cuisine is Mirchi hence our name. Yes – Mirchi can be HOT but at the same time we can tone it down and not be very Hot and Spicy for the general Mirchi customer. If you choose – we can be mildly spicy also – in the sense of tasteful flavorings instead of a fiery flavor. Basically the spice level is customizable for almost all of the dishes at Mirchi. Come taste and enjoy the authentic Indian Homestyle cuisine at Mirchi. At Mirchi we serve freshly prepared food. Mirchi takes pride in not utilizing too much of cooking oil. Yes – it is a very difficult task to achieve the authentic Indian taste at Mirchi while using less oil and being less greasy. We have been successfully serving excellent food without needing to have our preparations floating in a repulsive 1 inch thick layer of oil. Dine In, Carry Out, Catering, On-site services, Party and Banquet orders are all welcome. Try us out. Enjoy our hospitality. We wanna be your home away from home. Judge for yourselves.
