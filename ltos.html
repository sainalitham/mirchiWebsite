  • Frequently Asked Questions

    1. How do I began treatment?
      Answer: You can began treatment by calling our phone number and completing an intake with an office staff person following an assessment by our clinical staff.
    2. Do I have pay for services?
      Answer: No if you have Medicaid. Yes, if you have private insurance. (Please call to see if your carrier is accepted).
    3. Is there an age requirement to begin services?
      Answer: Yes, Ages 5 and above.
    4. What areas do you service?
      Answer: St. Tammany, Washington, and Tangipahoa parishes.
